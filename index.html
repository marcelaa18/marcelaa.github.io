Marcela Angel

Colombian architect interested in finding new ways in which architecture and urban design can generate social change, transform complex human settlements or maybe even an entire country. At DUSP, she wants to explore the role of urban planning in peace-building processes in developing countries through participatory design, data and spatial analysis. Her work experience ranges from architectural design at Felipe Uribe's studio and urban planning for the oil-company Pacific to public policy and strategy consultancy at an innovation lab.
